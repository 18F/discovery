About OASIS

The OASIS Family of Contract Vehicles

  • Specifically designed for the acquisition of professional services
  • Enables the use of ANY contract type
  • Separated into unrestricted (OASIS) and Small Business (OASIS SB) to make it easy to reserve task orders for small business categories
  • All vendors have DCAA-vetted financial systems
